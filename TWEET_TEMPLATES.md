## Tips for Your Tweet

1. **Tag important accounts**: @mastra, @hnginternship, @teleximapp
2. **Use relevant hashtags**: #HNGInternship #AI #Mastra #TypeScript
3. **Include visuals**: Screenshots, GIFs, or demo videos
4. **Add links**: GitHub repo, blog post, live demo
5. **Make it engaging**: Use emojis, but not too many
6. **Show personality**: Let your unique voice shine through
7. **Time it right**: Tweet when your audience is most active
